A brief introduction

Hi, I'm Feroz. I'm a software engineer or a computer scientist, depending on who you ask. I recently graduated with an MEng in Computing from Imperial College London.

I'm planning to put up some more information on the specific areas of Computing that interest me in the near future; at present I work on internet security (specifically anti-phishing services) at Netcraft. I have previously worked at France Telecom on network security, and at Imperial College, as part of the UROP and as a tutor to first year students in programming.

When I'm not working on code, I'm usually writing. I worked on the student paper at Imperial College for a few years, and was the Technology Editor for my final two years there. You can find some of my writing here.

In addition, I wrote a one-off piece for Denizens, which was (very surprisingly) picked up for publication by OUP in an English textbook for IB students.

At the moment (when not at work) I am taking lessons in and teaching myself Arabic, as well as planning my next writing project.
